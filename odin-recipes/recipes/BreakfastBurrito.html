David's Special Breakfast Burritos

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • 3 Eggs
  • Margarine
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat pan and place a tsp of Margarine inside
  2. Crack 3 eggs into pan and scramble them
  3. Put shredded cheese into eggs
  4. Put bacon into pan
  5. Place eggs and bacon into tortilla
  6. Put a few drops of hot sauce on top
  7. Fold tortilla
  8. Place burrito onto pan to sear the tortilla
  9. Enjoy!
